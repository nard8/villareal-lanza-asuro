Music Video Teaser Presentation
     

A music video, sometimes abbreviated to M/V, is a video that integrates a song or an album with imagery that is produced for promotional or musical artistic purposes. Many music videos interpret images and scenes from the song's lyrics, while others take a more thematic approach. Other music videos may not have any concept, being only a filmed version of the song's live concert performance.
